About

Hi, I'm Saumya.

By day, I work at a climate tech VC — reading decks, diligencing startups, thinking about how capital flows toward a less broken planet.

By night (and on weekends, and on the Metro, and honestly whenever), I write. It started in 8th grade with a poem that rhymed perfectly in ABAB and has since evolved into essays on culture, relationships, pop obsessions, and the strange corners of modern life.

I've written for The Hindu and Mint Lounge, and I keep a more personal archive on Substack and Medium. I write because I can't not.
